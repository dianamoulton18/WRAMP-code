Question 1/21


Imagine yourself participating in a game where you have to pick a marble from a set at random. Some of the marbles are pink, and some of them are purple. The game has two outcomes:
- If you pull a purple marble, you win.
- If you pull a pink marble, you lose.

In the pie chart, the purple section (darker color) represents the proportion of purple marbles in the set.


Your task: Observe the visualization and describe your chance of picking a purple marble and winning the game.
Do NOT use numerical answer. Instead, use familiar words and expressions to describe and communicate your chances.
